Safety Policy

Our team follows safety-first practices to protect clients, technicians, and property.

1. PPE & Safe Practices

Appropriate personal protective equipment and safe tool handling are required at all times.

2. Site Protection

Surfaces are protected as appropriate; debris is contained and removed per project needs.

3. Hazard Disclosure

Clients must disclose known hazards (e.g., lead paint, asbestos, mold). Work may be paused if hazards are present.

4. Family & Pet Safety

We coordinate with clients to keep children and pets away from active work areas.

5. Incident Reporting

Any incidents or near misses are promptly reported and reviewed to prevent recurrence.
